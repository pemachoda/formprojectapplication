HOME

Growing up in Beaconsfield and surrounding areas, we always thought there was a need for a restaurant with a different focus. In 2013 we opened the fun, casual bistro we always wanted to eat at and things quickly shifted towards creating an elevated experience, with a focus on farm-first, minimal waste dining. Our farm now supplies us with all of the fruit and vegetables used at the restaurant and bar. We have built great relationships with artisan producers from around Victoria, including Ruby Red Beef, Butterfly Factory, Bruthen Creek Pork, Corner Inlet Fishery, Mimosa Rock Oysters and Laucke Flour. Our menu changes daily, varying between 12 and 25 items.
